Created by the Design Thinking Initiative at Smith College, this project is the result of Smith students’ questions - Where can I make objects? Where can I learn new skills? We have a woodshop on campus? How do I gain access to these spaces? Our making spaces are scattered across campus but rich in resources - students of all majors have access to materials and tools from paint to CNC routers. This map is an effort to consolidate these resources into one living document.

Many people have contributed to the success of this project, with illustrations and design by Zoe Zandbergen ‘18, web development by Ali Eshghi ‘21 , photography by Samantha Grossman ‘22 and Isabelle Hodge ‘20, and project management by Laura Lilienkamp ‘18.
